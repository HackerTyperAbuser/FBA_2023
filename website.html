I. INTRODUCTION

- Future Business Analyst Season 3 is officially launching to
gather young people in the business analysis fields to showcase their skills in solving real-world business problems. With the theme Shooting Stars, we believe this year's contest will bring us the participants as our majestic meteors, shining brightly and casting their captivating glow, transcending boundaries while leaving their dazzling

CONTEST DETAILS
Competition Structure Competition Rules
FBA 2023 has 3 stages:
- Application form open (15/08 - 29/08)
- 3 rounds:
Round 1: Warm-up (online 31/08 - 06/09)
Round 2: Confrontation (online 11/09 - 17/09)
Round 3: Final (offline 23/09 - 01/10)
- Final Night		 - Each team consists of 5 participants, and contestants can register as a group or individually. The contestants will be supported by the organizers to match the team to the number of members in one team.
- Participating teams must pledge to provide accurate and complete information.
- The ideas of all entries must be original and not copied from any sources. The organizers will not be held responsible for violation of the copyright law by participants.
- Once the registration application is approved, teams cannot withdraw from the competition.
- The organizers are free to use submissions and the team members' information as needed, without any additional restrictions.
- Teams that perform any harmful action against the competition's conduct will be disqualified from participating in subsequent rounds.
- The organizers have the right to modify the contest's rules and forms. Accordingly, contestants will be informed of any changes 24 hours prior to the event
FBA organizers are always welcome constructive feedback and support from you all. Please follow us on our Facebook and website for the latest updates.
